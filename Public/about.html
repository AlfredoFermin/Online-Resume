Who am I?

My name is José Alfredo Fermin — though I go by Alfredo, which is why I abbreviate it to J. Alfredo. I'm a Software Engineering student at UAPA University in the Dominican Republic.

Beyond tech, I have a deep appreciation for the arts. Growing up, I studied at a music conservatory where I trained in opera singing and piano, and was also part of a choir. That love for creativity has stayed with me.

In my free time, you'll likely find me reading books on personal growth, drawing, or hitting the gym. I also enjoy going to the movies, trying new restaurants, spending time with friends, and getting out into nature — hiking included.
